### Task 3.2 — Silver Accounts Model with Quality Rules
**File:** `dbt/models/silver/silver_accounts.sql`

Implemented dbt model that:
- Applies two quality rules:
  - `NULL_REQUIRED_FIELD`: Rejects records where account_id, open_date, credit_limit, current_balance, billing_cycle_start, billing_cycle_end, or account_status is null/empty
  - `INVALID_ACCOUNT_STATUS`: Rejects records where account_status NOT IN ('ACTIVE', 'SUSPENDED', 'CLOSED')
- Writes rejected records to quarantine via post_hook with _rejection_reason codes
- Performs upsert on account_id: incoming records REPLACE existing records for same account_id
- Existing records not in incoming delta are retained
- Ensures exactly one record per account_id (assert before materialization)

**Upsert Logic:**
- Passing records from Bronze merged with existing Silver accounts
- Existing accounts without incoming delta are kept unchanged
- Incoming records replace stale records for same account_id

**Status:** ✅ COMPLETE
